CROSSY ROAD 2.0

Welcome to Crossy Road! In this game you can choose between three levels, which correspond to three different environments each with different types of challenges. The aim is to do as many steps as possible avoiding to hit any object. You can also choose between two different times of the day in the box below.

NOTE: WE ADVISE USING CHROME OR EDGE (CHROMIUM VERSION) FOR A BETTER EXPERIENCE.
Select the hour:

Easy level - Busy town

The rabbit has to cross a noisy and busy town, populated by large buildings, trees and speeding cars. You lose whenever you hit a vehicle.

Medium level - Snow bear

The polar bear must cross a snowy landscape studded with obstacles and icy expanses. You lose whenever you hit a moving obstacle or any fixed obstacle while the bear is sliding on ice. If you press the up arrow/W button while the bear is on ice, your speed will increase.

Hard level - Scary Halloween

The little girl quickly slips while sitting on the green halloween slime. You can start playing clicking the up arrow /W button and then you will not be able to stop. You lose whenever you hit any fixed or moving obstacle.

Tutorial - How to play

It is possible to move using the up/right/left arrows or the W/A/D buttons. Each step forward will increase your score by 1. In all levels, you will find some coins and each of them will allow you to earn 10 extra points.
